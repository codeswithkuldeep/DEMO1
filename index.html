About Balaji Glass Store

Three decades of shaping light, space & structure in Banda

For over 30 years, Balaji Glass Store has been Banda's trusted name for glass, UPVC, aluminium and complete interior fit-outs. From a single glass-cutting counter on Pandit Jain Road, we've grown into a full-service design and installation team serving homes, offices, showrooms and hospitals across the region — without ever compromising on material quality or finish.

Every panel we cut, every frame we fit, and every partition we install carries the same promise: precision measurement, premium-grade material, and a finish that holds up for decades, not just years.
